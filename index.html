Summary

In Pieces of Eight by Steve Goble, readers embark on a thrilling historical adventure with former pirate, Spider John. After nine long and arduous years at sea, Spider returns home, but not before facing one final challenge. Tragedy strikes when a crew member, Calvin Garrick, falls to his death, leading to suspicions of murder and a potential hijacking attempt. With the help of his friend Hob, Spider stops the hijacking and is rewarded. He sets sail for Nantucket, where he left his family years ago, only to be accused of murder upon arrival. With the help of his friends, Spider investigates the truth and ultimately discovers a sinister plot by his own in-laws to kill him.

A Spider John Mystery

Pieces of Eight is the latest installment in the four-part series centered around Spider John. This book takes readers on an exciting journey with Spider as he navigates the dangerous waters of his pirate past and confronts new obstacles in his quest to return home. The series has received praise for its captivating storyline and historical accuracy, making it a must-read for fans of historical fiction and adventure. The journey of Spider John continues in Pieces of Eight, offering fans of the series a thrilling and memorable experience.
