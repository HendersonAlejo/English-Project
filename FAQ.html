How did Spider John Get his name?

His name, Spider John, was earned through his daring feat of surviving the capture of the infamous pirate ship, Red Viper, and seeking justice for the loss of his best friend by killing the naval officer and spy responsible.

What happened to Spider John's family while he was gone?

While he was away, Spider John's wife faced a difficult time. She was forced into prostitution and later suffered physical abuse at the hands of Jeremy Stoneham. In an effort to escape this traumatic experience, she boarded the ship Margaret, captained by Samuel Wescott, and sailed to England with her son.

Who killed Calvin Garrick?

Calvin Garrick met a tragic end at the hands of Trawley, Gray, Simon, and Woodley, who ruthlessly took his life to preserve their secrecy in pursuit of a coveted treasure.

Who actually killed Jeremy Stoneham?

Jeremy Stoneham death was the result of a tragic accident caused by Spider John's brother-in-law, Enoch.

Who was cheating on their wife?

Mister Dawes, one of Spider Johns suspects in the killing of Jeremy Stoneham, was caught cheating on his wife during Spiders investigation.

How did Spider John's friends identify him in Nantucket?

Spider John has a distinctive habit of tilting his head back in a particular manner when he smokes, and this mannerism has become a telltale sign to his friends for recognizing him.

Who has a crush on Spider John?

Ruth has a crush on Spider John, but he is oblvious to the fact.

Will there be another book in the Spider John Mystery?

At the conclusion of the book, it becomes evident that the journey to find Spider John's family will be the focus of the next installment in the series. This is reinforced by the fact that the group unites in a vow to aid Spider John in his search. Their resolve is a testament to their loyalty and friendship, as they stand by his side, determined to uncover the truth about his past.
